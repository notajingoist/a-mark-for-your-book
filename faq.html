An answer for your question.

  • Q: Why are you doing this?

    A: I don't know... I like making bookmarks. And I've always wanted to sell crafts and things, but I feel like no one would actually pay me for them.

  • Q: Can I have more than one bookmark?

    A: Yes, but be reasonable please... Uh, let's set an arbitrary upper limit of one granted bookmark request per day. As in, you may send me a billion email requests, but at most I'll only pay attention to one of them.

  • Q: Do you promise to make me a bookmark?

    A: No.

  • Q: Are requests first come first serve?

    A: Generally yes, unless I like someone else more. Then they get the bookmark, obviously.

  • Q: Do you make anything other than bookmarks?

    A: Sure! Let me know if you want a card or something instead.

  • Q: Are these really free?

    A: Yes. But if you insist on giving me money, please consider assisting me with my stamp & envelope fund!

  • Q: Who are you?

    A: My name is Jing.

  • Q: Can I send you something?

    A: Omgosh yesss! If you aren't a sketchy stalker, feel free to email me and I will happily (but warily) give you my snail mail address.
